About

Brandt Wrestling is built for athletes who want to learn the right way, train with purpose, and keep leveling up.

Coaching focus

Fundamentals first. We build from stance, motion, and defense into controlled offense and chain wrestling.

Room standards

Show up, work hard, be a good teammate. No ego. No excuses. Bring energy and respect.

Training style

High reps, smart drilling, live goes with intention. We compete to improve, not to posture.
